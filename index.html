Trip Split is here to save the day... or at least make it fair.

Why use Trip Split?

Planning on taking a road trip with some friends or family? Our app was designed to split the cost and notify the amount owed to whom it is assigned. It is a great way to keep track of your spending on a trip, and avoiding a situation where one person ends up paying way more than others because of unexpected expenses. At the end of the day, it is nice to have one less thing keeping you up at night.

Our app makes it easy to monitor the spending from a trip. With features like payment schedules, adding to the trip total with pictures of receipts and adding unlimited amount of people to share the costs by percentage. You can pay off with cash and manually update the amount owed, or our app provides fast and secure bank to bank transfers and credit card payments.The layout was designed to be easy and intuitive for everyone to use.
